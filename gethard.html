Get Hard

A must see Comedy

"Get Hard" is a 2015 comedy film that stars Kevin Hart and Will Ferrell. The movie tells the story of James King, a millionaire who is sentenced to prison for a crime he didn't commit. In a panic, he turns to Darnell Lewis, a car wash attendant played by Kevin Hart, for help in preparing him for life behind bars.

As James' training begins, it becomes clear that Darnell has no experience with prison life, but he is willing to do whatever it takes to help James. The duo embarks on a series of hilarious and misguided attempts to get James ready for prison, from learning how to fight to simulating prison yard scenarios.

"Get Hard" is a fun and light-hearted comedy that pokes fun at the stereotypes and misconceptions about prison life. The film's success is largely due to the excellent chemistry between the two leads, Kevin Hart and Will Ferrell. Their comedic styles play off each other perfectly, and they deliver several laugh-out-loud moments throughout the movie.
