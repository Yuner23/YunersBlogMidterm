Ride Along

Action Comedy

"Ride Along" is a 2014 action-comedy film that follows the story of Ben Barber, a security guard who dreams of becoming a police officer. He goes on a ride-along with his girlfriend's brother, James Payton, who is a tough and seasoned cop. Ben hopes to prove himself to James and earn his blessing to marry his sister. However, the ride-along turns into a high-stakes criminal investigation that puts their lives in danger.

The movie showcases the chemistry between the two leads, Kevin Hart and Ice Cube. Kevin Hart's humor adds a light-hearted touch to the action-packed film, and Ice Cube's tough-guy persona balances it out with a more serious tone. The dynamic between the two actors makes for an entertaining and enjoyable film.

"Ride Along" is a great example of a buddy cop movie that delivers both laughs and action. The film has a solid storyline, and the characters' development throughout the movie keeps the audience engaged. Overall, "Ride Along" is a fun movie that is worth watching, especially if you are a fan of Kevin Hart or Ice Cube.


The move had a continuation which is "Ride Along 2" a 2016 action-comedy film and a sequel to the first "Ride Along" movie. The film picks up where the first one left off, with Ben Barber (Kevin Hart) now a police officer and James Payton (Ice Cube) still a detective. The duo travels to Miami to track down a drug lord (Benjamin Bratt) who has been supplying drugs to Atlanta.

As they investigate the case, Ben and James find themselves in the middle of a conspiracy involving corrupt cops and the drug lord's sister, Maya (Olivia Munn). With high-speed chases, shootouts, and hilarious banter, the two officers must work together to take down the bad guys and save the day.

The movie features the same comedic chemistry between Kevin Hart and Ice Cube that made the first "Ride Along" film such a success. The addition of new characters, including Olivia Munn as Maya and Ken Jeong as a hacker named AJ, adds to the fun and excitement of the movie. The film also offers plenty of action and suspense, making it a great choice for fans of the buddy-cop genre.
